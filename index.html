Carbon Market Taxonomy
Compliance Carbon Markets (CCM)
Legally mandated cap-and-trade systems operated by government bodies. Scarcity engineered via annual Linear Reduction Factor. Penalties for non-compliance up to €100/tCO₂. Institutional-grade liquidity.
EU ETSUK ETSCalifornia CCARGGIKorea ETS
Voluntary Carbon Markets (VCM)
Corporate CSR-driven offset purchases beyond mandatory compliance thresholds. Third-party verified by Gold Standard, Verra VCS, or ACR. Project-specific risk adjustments. Lower base liquidity vs CCM.
Verra VCSGold StandardACRCAR
EU
EU ETS — Core System
World's longest-running carbon market. Covers ~40% of EU GHG emissions across power, industry, and aviation. Phase 4 (2021–30) applies 4.3% LRF from 2024 under Fit for 55 revisions.

  • High liquidity · institutional-grade

CSR-driven offset purchases

  • Verra VCS · Gold Standard · ACR · CAR

How Pricing Works ◆ Fixed vs Increment
Fixed Price Mechanisms~40-47 systems

Government sets the price per ton. Certainty over cuts.

Examples
🇲🇽 Mexico · 🇨🇴 Colombia · 🇿🇦 South Africa · 🇨🇳 China (some pilots)

Cap shrinks yearly via the LRF. Scarcity drives price.
